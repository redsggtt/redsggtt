ALUCARD

. Alucard is a very popular hero because he is easy to use. You don't need to have high mechanics because using Alucard doesn't need that. Just need to jump in and fight it out basically. He is very strong when 1v1 and can be even stronger when fighting many enemies at once

HAYABUSA

Hayabusa is a killer hero with a high explosive attack, this attack comes from the highest Shadow Kill skill and 1 Phantom Shuriken skill, which provides a powerful melee explosion attack. In a one-on-one situation, this hero has a great chance to win.

POPOL AND KUPA

Popol and Kupa are Marksman heroes who have very strong resistance. This Marksman Hero has a wide range of skills that makes it difficult to beat. Of course Popol and Kupa can become very deadly Marksman heroes if these heroes are used correctly.
